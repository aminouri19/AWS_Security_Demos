KPIs to Measure Cloud Governance and Automation

1- Percent of policies in compliant state (%)
2- Cost optimized over time ($)
3- Cost optimized per policy over time ($)
4- Time saved as a result of policies (time)
5- Number of reservations automated (n)
6- Time to remediate security violations (time)
7- Service availability (%)
8- Time to deployment (time)
