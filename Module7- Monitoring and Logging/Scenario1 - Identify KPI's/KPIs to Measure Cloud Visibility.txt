KPIs to Measure Cloud Visibility

1- Percent of environment with proper tagging in place (%)
2- Percent of total bill charged back (%)
3- Variance of budget vs. actual by application or team (%) 
4- Forecast accuracy (%)
5- Security incident per month by team (n)
6- Security vulnerabilities identified per month per team (n)
7- Mean time for vulnerability announced to all systems patched (n)
