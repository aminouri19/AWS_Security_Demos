KPI's to Measure Cloud Optimization

1- Percentage of infrastructure running ‘on-demand’ (vs. covered by Reservation, Savings Plan, Committed Use Discount, etc.) (%)
2- Rightsizing savings (%)
3- Effective cost per resource (i.e., $/compute hour) ($)
4- Production incidents by application/team (n)
5- Reverted deploys (%)
6- Mean time to repair or mean time between failures (time)
7- Number of security lapses (open ports, IAM failures, etc.) (n)
8- Number of assets that don’t meet configuration standards (wrong VM type, location, image, OS, tagging) (n)
