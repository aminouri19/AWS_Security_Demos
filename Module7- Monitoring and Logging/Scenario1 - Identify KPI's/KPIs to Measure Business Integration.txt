KPIs to Measure Business Integration

1- Cost per customer ($)
2- Cloud spend as a percentage of revenue (%)
3- Reduction in Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) over time (%)
4- Cost of revenue over time ($)
5- Time to bring new services to market (time) 
6- Compliance issues open (n)
7- Mean time to detect (time) 
8- Customer satisfaction (typically using Net Promoter Score)
